## ASSIGNMENT

Loss Solutions Group was contacted by Mr. Brad Vifquain of Hanover Insurance. The scope of this assignment was to address the reparability, replacement cost value (RCV), and actual cash value (ACV) of a Lincoln Electric fume extraction unit (smoke eater) located at Precision Wire Forms, Inc., 1100 West Broadway, Three Rivers, MI 49093. The unit reportedly sustained fire damage on January 14, 2026. Documents and photos were provided for a desk review.

## FINDINGS AND ANALYSIS

Loss Solutions Group contacted the insured, Mr. Mark Lillywhite of Precision Wire Forms, Inc., at (269) 273-1976. Mr. Lillywhite reported that the smoke eater is used to extract welding fumes from a weld cell in their facility. He stated the unit has a filter system and that preventative maintenance was being performed on the equipment. Mr. Lillywhite reported that the wiring, fan speed controls, VFD, relays, and other internal components were melted due to the fire. He confirmed the unit was approximately two (2) years old and was put into service in October 2025. Mr. Lillywhite stated he would provide additional details on the unit, including make, model, and serial number information.

Loss Solutions Group contacted Mr. Mark Coble of Airgas USA, LLC, at (260) 888-9091. Mr. Coble is an account specialist who prepared the quote for the insured. He confirmed that the insured obtained the quote but had not placed the order. Mr. Coble stated that he had seen the unit and confirmed it was beyond repair due to the extent of heat and melting damage. He quoted the exact same unit that was currently in the insured's plant. Airgas USA, LLC provided Quotation #2014146135 dated January 21, 2026, in the amount of $32,771.33.

Additionally, we received a verbal quote from Welding Mart Mr. Jeremiah, at (877) 532-9353. Mr. Jeremiah contacted Lincoln Electric directly and called back with a price of $29,000.00 for a new Lincoln Electric Co Prism 4 Compact (10 HP, 4000 CFM) fume extraction unit with inlet upwards, SKU# AD2455-2. This further validated that the cost presented to us is fair and reasonable.

A total amount of $32,771.33 was presented.

## SCOPE OF DAMAGE

Loss Solutions Group was asked to review fire damage to one (1) Lincoln Electric Co Prism 4 Compact fume extraction unit (smoke eater) that reportedly sustained damage on January 14, 2026.

## AGE & CONDITION

Based on information gathered during the investigation, the Lincoln Electric fume extraction unit was approximately two (2) years old and was put into service in October 2025. Preventative maintenance was reportedly being performed on the equipment. Loss Solutions Group has assessed the condition of the equipment as average at the time of the loss.

## CAUSE OF LOSS

The cause of loss was reported as fire. Loss Solutions Group was not tasked with determining the cause of the loss for this claimed event.

## REPARABILITY

Based on the photos and documentation reviewed, the internal electrical components of the fume extraction unit, including the wiring, VFD, relays, and fan speed controls, were extensively melted and damaged by fire. The vendor, Airgas USA, LLC, confirmed the unit was beyond repair. Loss Solutions Group concurs that replacement is warranted.
