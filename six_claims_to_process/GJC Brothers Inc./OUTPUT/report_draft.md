## ASSIGNMENT

Loss Solutions Group was contacted by Ms. Tina Taveras of Travelers Insurance. ## FINDINGS AND ANALYSIS

Loss Solutions Group contacted Mr. Charlie Audi of GJC Brothers Inc. (insured) at (617) 365-4184. Mr. Audi stated that the car wash was installed in 2020 and that NCS is the company he purchased the car wash from. NCS services the car wash on a monthly maintenance subscription. Mr. Audi reported that the car wash is currently running but not at full capacity, and that he is waiting on the insurance company before proceeding with repairs. He noted that parts may take two (2) to three (3) weeks to arrive once ordered.

## SCOPE OF DAMAGE

The claimed equipment consists of a Ryko Slimline Dryer and exterior fascia/sign cover at a car wash facility. The equipment reportedly sustained damage from a vehicle impact on February 5, 2026. The damage includes a cracked and broken exterior fascia panel, damaged fan nozzle assemblies, associated mounting hardware, air hose line, flexible hose, wire mesh, clamps, sleeves, and bushings.

## AGE & CONDITION

Per the manufacturer data plate, the Ryko Slimline Dryer was manufactured in July 2020, making it approximately six (6) years old at the time of loss. The insured reported that the car wash is serviced on a monthly maintenance subscription by NCS. Based on the age of the equipment and the reported regular maintenance, Loss Solutions Group considers the equipment to have been in average condition at the time of loss.

## CAUSE OF LOSS

The damage was reportedly caused by a vehicle (large sprinter van) that struck the car wash dryer and exterior fascia/sign cover while driving through and exiting the car wash on February 5, 2026. Photos provided depict impact damage to the fascia panel and fan nozzle components consistent with a vehicle strike.

## REPARABILITY

Based on a review of the provided documentation and the repair quote from NCS, Loss Solutions Group determines that the damaged components can be repaired/replaced to restore the car wash dryer to pre-loss condition. Replacement of the damaged parts is the appropriate course of action.

## ACTUAL CASH VALUE (ACV)

The Ryko Slimline Dryer was manufactured in July 2020, making it approximately six (6) years old at the time of loss. Loss Solutions Group applied a useful life expectancy of twenty (20) years for commercial car wash dryer components. The depreciation percentage is calculated as six (6) years divided by twenty (20) years, which equals thirty percent (30%). Materials have been depreciated at 30%. Labor, travel, and fuel surcharge are not subject to depreciation.

Based on the above, Loss Solutions Group's actual cash value for this claimed event totals **$4,332.15** (including applicable sales tax), reflecting total depreciation of $1,120.23. Please refer to the attached spreadsheet for a detailed breakdown.
